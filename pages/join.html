Become a Partner

Partners help make ION Racing a reality, they are our greatest supporters!

Should you be interested, please feel free to contact us via email or by filling out the application form below. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to reach out!

Why should you become a partner?

Investment in Future Talent

By supporting ION, partners give students the opportunity to gain knowledge, skills, and experiences that can contribute to their future careers.

Networking and Relationship Building

We arrange events throughout the year, creating a platform to network and build relationships with students, faculty members, and other partners. These connections can lead to future collaborations, partnerships, or potential hires.

Meet potential future employees

Partners can leverage their involvement to identify potential candidates for internships, entry-level positions and future hires.

Become a Crewmember

You can send us an email or apply directly by filling out our application form. We hope to hear from you!

What can Ion offer you as a student?

Having Fun and Enjoying University Life

Engaging in student organizations provides an opportunity to have fun, meet new people, and enjoy a more fulfilling university experience.

Skill Development

Being a part of ION may give you a platform to enhance various skills beyond academic learning. Through participation, you may get the chance to develop skills like leadership, communication, teamwork, time management and more.

Networking

Joining ION Racing provides you with an opportunity to expand your social and professional networks. Connect with peers who share similar interests and build relationships with faculty and industry professionals through networking events and activities.

Enhancing Your Resume

Active involvement in student organizations shows to potential employers that a student is proactive and capable of managing multiple responsibilities.
